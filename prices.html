Cake prices start with 6". $15 and up to $50 depending on what icing or fondant is used; layered or single layer cake.

8" & 10" are $75 and up; also depends on single layer or layered up to 4 layers; depends on designs whether they are fondant, sugar, gum paste, or real flowers.

12" $100 and up; each layer will be priced separately depending on icing or fondant use to cover cake.

Wedding cakes will cost between $400 and up; can be as much as $5000 and all depends on the design of the cake, how many layers (if any), and if to be delivered or picked up.

All orders are required to have half down when order is taken.
